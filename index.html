            What these numbers mean
            
      Headline totals for the whole file. Resolved counts tickets in a closed state (Closed, Resolved, or Ready to Close). Median times sit next to the averages because a handful of very long tickets can pull an average upward, so the median is usually the more honest "typical" figure. Avg interactions is the mean of Freshdesk's Customer interactions count — roughly how many replies/messages were exchanged on a ticket, so it's a proxy for how much back-and-forth each one needed (higher = more effort per ticket).
      
          
      

          
          
      
            
      
              
      Ticket volume (created)
      
              
      
                
                
              
      
            
      
            
      
              What this shows
              
      How many tickets were created in each period, by the ticket's created date. Toggle Per day / Per week to smooth out day-to-day noise and see the broader trend. Weeks start on Monday; hover a bar for the exact count.
      
            
      
            
      
          
      

          
          
      
            
      Agent performance
      
            
      
              What this shows
              
      One row per agent. Tickets is the total assigned to them; Resolved is how many reached a closed state. Median first-response and resolution times reflect speed, and avg interactions reflects how much back-and-forth their tickets needed. Compare median resolution across agents rather than the average, since one stuck ticket can distort a mean.
      
            
      
            
      
          
      

          
          
      
            
      
              
      First-response time
      
              
      
                What this shows
                
      How long the first reply took, grouped into time bands. Weighted toward the left (under a few hours) is healthy — a large "> 24h" bar points to tickets waiting too long for an initial touch.
      
              
      
              
      
            
      
            
      
              
      Resolution time
      
              
      
                What this shows
                
      How long tickets took to fully resolve, in bands. A long tail (over 3 days) is worth digging into — it's often a few complex or escalated tickets.
      
              
      
              
      
            
      
          
      

          
          
      
            
      Customer satisfaction — survey by priority
      
            
      
              What this shows
              
      Survey ratings on the 5-point scale (Excellent → Below Expectations), broken down by ticket priority. Only tickets the customer actually rated are counted, so each row also shows the response rate. The average score maps Excellent = 5 down to Below Expectations = 1.
